Wasabi Mashed Pototoes

Description

Mashed Pototoes are wonderful. Can't argue this. Now add some spice and you've entered an entirely different realm. Wasabi, typically put on sushi is a great additive to mashed pototoes. Especially if you are paring the dish with a Seared Ahi dish.

Making wasabi mashed potatoes isn't all that different from your typical mashed potatoes. Same style, only you add wasabi at the end. Easy enough.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium sized gold potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Wasabi Powder

Steps

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into small pieces
  2. Boil potatoes until soft
  3. Mash potatoes with large flat spoon
  4. Put wasabi powder into small bowl with a little water
  5. Mix wasabi into a thick consistency
  6. Add butter to the mashed potatoes
  7. Add salt and pepper for taste
  8. Finally, thoroughly mix in wasabi to preferred liking
