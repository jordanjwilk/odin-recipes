Seared Ahi

Description

Seared Yellow Fin Tuna, commonly known as "Ahi" is one of the best tasting fish there is. It can be eaten fully cooked or fully raw. Both are an excellent choice.

Cooking and searing are great options however,Ahi is also used to make several dishes such as poke and Poisson Cru where it is cut into small chuncks and mixed with different vegetables and dressings.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. piece of Ahi
  • Sesame oil
  • Furikake seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat a stainless steal pan to medium
  2. Pour enough Sesame oil to cover most of the pan
  3. Place Ahi in pan and sear on each side for no more than 1 minute
  4. Remove Ahi when could to satisfaction
  5. Slice and Serve
