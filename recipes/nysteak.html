New York Steak

Description

New York steaks are a favorite among many. The versatile cut can be used and cooked in a variety of different dishes and appliances. One the easiest and fastest ways to ensure an even temp is by cooking in oven at 400 degrees Farenheight.

In what follows, we will show you some of the simplest ways to enjoy this wonderful steak.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz New York Steak
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheight
  2. Season Steak with salt and butter
  3. Heat cast iron on stove top
  4. Sear all sides of steak for 1-2 minutes
  5. Place cast iron in the oven
  6. Set timer to 8 minutes
  7. Remove steak and let sit for 10 minutes
  8. Slice and serve
